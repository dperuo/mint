We don’t believe we can predict which new business will work and which will fail.

Instead of predicting, we strive to maximise the rate of experiments getting real world feedback. We minimise meetings, strategic discussion, and gatekeepers.

It’s a highly devolved structure. Everyone is encouraged to launch new businesses. There's no management direction about which ones get further investment: instead Mints flock to the projects where they think they can add most value.

Every new beginning comes from some other beginning’s end.
